About Me

Hi, I'm Yiunfan Hu. I am currently an undergrad mechanical engineering student at Stony Brook University. I primarily like to design and build things. My current obsessions, and most recent projects, are in electric vehicles and combat robots.

I am self taught in CAD and started learning with my first 3D printer in late middle school. Throughout high school, I was a part of an FIRST Robotics team, helped assist several STEM classes, and started building combat robotics with a small team of friends.

Apart from mechanical engineering adjacent skills, I am also proficient in coding and basic electronics.
